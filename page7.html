TABLE TENNIS

RULES & REGULATIONS:

Match will be in between colleges in a single, single and doubles manner.

College team may have 3-4 players and one of the singles match player can play doubles too.

Doubles will have cross serve and cross playoff, Service for singles is non cross type.

All matches till semifinals will be of 10 and 1 game point.

Lucky service will be considered a re-serve and player will get 2 re-serves.

Non playing team members of all players should be 5 ft away from table. Otherwise single player of the same team will be disqualified.

Banging of table after serve will be considered a foul play resulting in loss of 1 point.

Semi finals will be of 15 and 1 game point, whereas finals will be of 20 and 1 game point. Deuce will last till 10 points, after which there will be a golden point.

RULES FOR BOTH BOYS & GIRLS ARE THE SAME.
